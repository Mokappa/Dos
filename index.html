Each player receives 5 cards.

The starting player has to place a card of the same color or value as the card on the table. If a player has several cards of the same color or value, he may place them.

If the player cannot or doesn't want to place a card, he has to draw one from the pack.

If the value of the card on the table is 2 or 3, the next player must draw 2 or 3 cards.

The player with 2 cards in his hand will say "DOS".

The winner is the player who has no more cards in his hand.
